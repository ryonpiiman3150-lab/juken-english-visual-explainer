# Reading and Interpretation Module

Use this module for long passages, sentence interpretation, translation, content questions, summaries, and multiple-choice reading tasks.

## Reading Priorities

- Identify the main claim and paragraph roles.
- Show logical markers: contrast, cause, example, concession, addition, and conclusion.
- Locate evidence for each answer.
- Distinguish direct evidence from inference.
- Explain why distractors fail when choices are present.

## Sentence Interpretation

- Identify the main clause first.
- Mark clause boundaries and modifiers.
- Show what each phrase modifies.
- Resolve pronouns and omitted elements.
- Provide a natural Japanese interpretation after the structure is clear.

## Useful UI Patterns

- Toggleable highlights for `主節`, `従属節`, `修飾`, `論理語`, `指示語`.
- Paragraph map showing each paragraph's role.
- Evidence links from questions to passage lines.
- Choice-comparison panel with `根拠`, `ずれ`, `判断`.
- Sentence tree or bracket view when structure is genuinely complex.

## Avoid

- Translating every word before showing the logical structure.
- Explaining choices without pointing to evidence.
- Over-annotating simple sentences.
